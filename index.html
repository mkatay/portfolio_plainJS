About me

I am a teacher, and I am delighted to teach one of the most beautiful and fascinating subjects: web programming. I have the privilege of working with intelligent, talented, and dedicated young people. I teach both frontend and backend development, striving to impart knowledge of the latest and most modern technologies.

Web programming gives you the opportunity to bring your ideas to life. Whether you want to create a blog, an online store, or a full-fledged social platform, the knowledge we acquire here will open every door for you. Moreover, the joy of creative expression, problem-solving, and creation are all part of this journey.

Every website you visit, every app you use, is someone's work who once started exactly where you are now. And now you can embark on this exciting journey too! This is not just a skill, but an opportunity to create, develop, and even change the world. The future is digital, and you will be the builders of this future.
